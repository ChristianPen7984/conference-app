ABOUT US

Here at Conference.io, our goal is to provide individuals with the best experience when searching for conferences around the world. It is of our pleasure to allow access to the most efficient information regarding particular conferences of interest. Typically, an individual would spend a considerable amount of time browsing the web for various conferences. However, we solve this problem by providing them all within a singular resource. Users have the ability to browse our continously expanding conglomeration of conferences with ease. Several pieces of data regarding a conference are provided including: name, location, and date.
